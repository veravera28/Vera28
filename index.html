About us

-

Soap is a product that is used for personal hygiene and to clean certain objects. It can be found in pills, powder, lotion, or liquid. Our idea is to make a soap based on less harmful products than coomon soaps.

-

Common soap contains many additives and chemical components that are unnecessary and harmful to the skin. It is made from aloe vera, being beneficial for the skin. This idea was to support the third objective of the ONU called Good Health and Well-being.

-

The soap contains this following products: pulps of 2 branches of Aloe Vera, reiterate the skin, rosemary essential oil to flavor it, 750 grams of olive oil, 95 grams of caustic soda (sodium hydroxide), 234 grams of water.

-

We made this Aloe Vera soap with the intention of reducing the use of products with many chemicals and also increasing personal hygiene since in Mexico only 34% of children and 60% of adults wash thir hands, and hygiene it has only one objective, to preserve health and prevent disease.

-

Aloe Vera is a thorny plant. It originated in eastern and southern Africa. There are more than 250 identified species, but it is the most popular. It has been used as a folk remedy for various ailments, burns, and even to improv the appearance of the skin, due to its many observed biological properties.

-

Preventing hair loss, relieving burns, were some of the many uses for wich aloe vera was used in greco-roman times.

-

This interest in hair in ancient Greece led to thorough stufy of methods to combat hair loss. To try to combat hair loss, Hippocrates himself created a remedy together with Pedanio Discorides Anazarbeo, a renowned doctor, pharmacologist and botanist, who recommended the external use of Aloe Vera.
